Welcome to Good Samaritan School

The Good Samaritan School prioritizes building into the younger generation while they are in the school. The youth are the future leaders and decision makers of the world and it's with this knowledge the staff wholeheartedly pour their efforts into caring for and teaching the students.

Good Samaritan School is a co-educational English Medium Senior Secondary School, recognized by the Directorate of Education and affiliated to CBSE. It is established and managed by The Good Samaritans, a Christian minority charitable society.

Learn More

Mission Statement

The Good Samaritan School is committed to serve underprivileged children in the love of Christ:

To provide quality education in accordance with the standards set by the Government,

To teach discipline, respect, honesty, self-reliance, perseverance and love for one another,

To provide a safe and creative learning environment,

To encourage an ideal student-teacher relationship, and
To prepare students to become contributing citizens of the nation and privileged partners in this mission.
